TMT REBARS

TMT bars or Thermo-Mechanically Treated bars are high-strength reinforcement bars having a tough outer core and a soft inner core. The very first step of the manufacturing process involves passing the steel wires through a rolling mill stand.

WIRE RODS

Wire rods are manufactured in special steel grades also for various applications like Coldheading. Tyre-bead, Cable armouring, Electrodes, Pre-stressed Concrete wire etc.,

SQUARE BARS

Square Bar is a solid metal bar that has a square cross section. These bars are widely used in many industries for general assembly or manufacturing. They are also used for general repairs of plant equipment and railings. Common applications include ornamental iron work, gates and protective barriers on windows.
